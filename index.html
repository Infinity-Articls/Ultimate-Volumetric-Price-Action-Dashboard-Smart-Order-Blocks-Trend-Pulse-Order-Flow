Chapter 1: Smart Order Blocks (The Engine)

An Order Block (OB) is not just a candle; it's a zone of institutional transaction intensity. Our algorithm uses a unique Volume-Weighted Pivot system to identify these zones.

Detection Precision

We use ta.pivothigh() and ta.pivotlow() but with a twist: we apply them to the Volume Series. This ensures a zone only appears if a massive spike in orders occurred.

Equilibrium Logic

The code calculates the avg (Mid-Point) of every zone. This is the "Fair Value" where institutions look to reload their positions.

Chapter 2: Zero-Lag Momentum Pulse

Lag is the greatest killer of trading performance. To solve this, we implemented a Zero-Lag Differentiator. Instead of waiting for a moving average to turn, we calculate the rate of change using a recursive WMA subtraction model.

The Mathematical Proof

Lag Reduction Formula pulse = 3 * ta.wma(close, L) - 2 * ta.wma(ta.wma(close, L), L)

This formula amplifies recent price changes while neutralizing the "drag" effect of older data. When the pulse turns Teal, you are entering the trend at the exact moment of institutional buy-side confirmation.
